Creative Cake decoration.

 

Hi and thank you for stopping by. 

My name is Marisa Kemp - a multi award-winning cake designer and decorator based in Beckenham, South East London.

I specialise in bespoke, novelty, intricate fun cakes and toppers with over a decade of experience in advanced techniques such as royal icing, sugar and flower paste, cake lace, pastillage, modelling, sculpting, isomalt and painting. 

I run small and one to one classes. See the classes section if you are interested.

Please look through the galleries to see some of my work. You can get the description of each piece or cake by clicking on each photo.

My hope is to inspire you to get the cake that you dream of.

Feel free to get in touch to talk about a cake or course of your choice.

With love, Marisa.
