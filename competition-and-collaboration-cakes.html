Black Masquerade

This collaboration piece was inspired by the Black Masquerade, one of the Divas illustrations created by the acclaimed and talented Fairy and Fantasy Artist: “Maxine Gadd”.

This is a structured bust cover with homemade chocolate and sugar paste. For the decorations, I used gumpaste, sugarpaste, royal icing, isomalt and cake lace. Totally edible, with exception of the wired flowers.

It was a real pleasure to take part in The Sugar Myths and Fantasies Global Edition collaboration alongside over 120 talented artists from all over the world!!

Engagement Cake

Celebration entry for Cake International Alexander Palace London, awarded Silver.

2 tier covered in sugarpaste, decorated with royal icing and some sugarpaste daises on a pastillage vase on top.

The royal icing decorations include some lambeth technique, scrolls, and line and string work, with run-out designs. 

Wedding Cake

3 tier Wedding Cake entry for Squires Kitchen Competition 2016.

This is covered in sugarpaste and decorated with royal icing details including some lambeth technique, scrolls, line and run-out work and some pastillage for the gazebo. For the steainless steal windows effect, I used some piping gel around royal icing, modelling paste for the bride and groom owl models and gumpaste for the flowers inside of the pastillage vase, inside the gazebo.

I was awarded a 'Commended" certificate for this cake.

Celebration Cake

A single tier celebration cake entry for Cake International Excel London 2015.

Cake covered in sugarpaste, decorated with royal icing, bridge and line work, a sugarpaste designed panel underneith, some deises and butterflies made with gumpaste and an abstract semi circle royal icing work on top of cake.

I was awarded a Bronze certificate.

Old Hollywood Glamour Wedding Cake

This was my entry for Squires Kitchen Competition 2015.

2 tier cake covered in sugarpaste and decorated with royal icing and some pastillage for the stairs, covered with gumpaste and decorated with royal icing. The couple on top is hand modelled with modelling paste, dressed with gumpaste and royal icing string work for the brides hair ornament.

I was awarded First Price on this class with this cake!

Miniature Wedding Cake

My entry for Squires Kitchen Competition 2015.

2 tier miniature cake (to fit within 6" square), covered in sugarpaste and decorated with royal icing, with some lambeth technique, bridge, line and string work, with 3 piped red roses.

I was awarded a "Commended" certificate with this cake.

Bald Ballerina for "I Guerrieri Collaboration"

This was my piece for “I Guerrieri” Collaboration, inspired by Maggie Kudirka, whom at the age of 23 and a dancer for the Joffrey Concert Group in NYC, in 2014, was diagnosed with metastatic (stage 4) breast cancer. After undergone chemotherapy and double mastectomy, she was back in the dance studio only 6 days after surgery. Maggie and her family set up a donation website to help with her mounting expenses, which she will need for the rest of her life or until a cure is found. She teaches Ballet master classes and shares her story to raise awareness and funds for breast cancer treatment and research.
